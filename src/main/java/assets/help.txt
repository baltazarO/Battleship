How to play the game:
 1. Place all 3 ships on your board
 2. Click enemy board to attack
 3. Game ends when all ships for 1 player are sunk

 Have fun!

Extra Features:
Captain's Quarters. Marked in green on ships. 
For the size 2 ship it will sink when the captain's quarters are hit.
The 2 larger ships have armored captain's quarters but will sink once 
the captain's quarters are hit twice.

Sonar Pulse. After sinking the first enemy ship you will unlock the 
ability to use 2 sonar pulses. These reveal a small portion of the 
enemy board. Grey is empty space, black is ships. The pulse will dissapear 
once you attack, so be careful!
